Spread Wings Spread Hope (SWSH)

Spread Wings Spread Hope (SWSH) is a social initiative inspired by the Ukrainian word 'Metelyk' (butterfly). We sell butterfly-themed t-shirts to support humanitarian causes, with a focus on aiding those affected by conflict and hardship. Our mission is to spread hope and make a tangible difference in communities in need.

To buy a shirt, reach out to alan@themetelyk.com

MixMatch

MixMatch is a dynamic sports tracking app designed for friends to connect, compete, and track their progress in various sports like tennis and basketball. It offers clean, intuitive statistics to help users analyze their performance and enjoy friendly rivalries.
